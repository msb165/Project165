[h1]Introduction[/h1]

[b]Project 165[/b] is a general content mod with the goal of expanding and improving the overall game experience.

[h2]Content[/h2]

This mod features:

[list]
[*] 3 new bosses
[*] 3 new enemies
[*] 44 new items
[/list]

Besides adding new content, this mod also does some alterations and improvements to the vanilla game, here's what it changes:

[list]
[*] Improves the [i]Frost Legion[/i] event and moves it to pre-Hardmode.
[*] Makes the [i]Chlorophyte Saber[/i] a true melee weapon, only spawning projectiles uppon hitting an enemy.
[*] Enchances [i]Frost Bolt Staff[/i]'s projectile visuals.
[*] Enchances [i]Influx Waver[/i]'s projectile visuals.
[*] Changes some of the gem staves to make them a bit different from each other.
[*] Increases [i]Terragrim[/i]'s drop chance from 2% to 50%.
[*] Makes the hold animation of staves smoother.
[/list]

And much more!
